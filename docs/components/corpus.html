corpus — rule/skill/memory storage + Zod reader

Tier: Storage · Files: src/server/corpus/reader.ts · rules/ · skills/ · memories/ · .betterai/ · Last commit: f4f8801 (Wave 3)

Markdown-on-disk corpus. Two scopes: global (~/.betterai/) and repo (<repo-root>/.betterai/). Reader parses frontmatter + body, validates with Zod, applies id-collision override at load time (repo wins). Boot-time validation is the contract that lets agents trust the response.
